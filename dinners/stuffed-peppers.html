Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 4 peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 800g chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp. tomato ketchup
  • Some oregano springs
  • Small bunch of basil or parsley
  • 100g grated cheese (optional)
  • A drizzle of oil

Instructions

Time to prepare: 10 minutes
Time to cook: 45 minutes

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 200°C (180°C fan). Heat the oil in a large pan.
  2. Step 2: Add the onion and cook it in the pan for a few minutes until it softens. Add the garlic and beef, and cook until the meat is brown. Break it up as it cooks.
  3. Step 3: Add the tomatoes, oregano, beef stock cube, and ketchup. Season, stir everything, and cover with a lid. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Cut the peppers in half, scoop out the seeds and membrane. Place them cut-side up in a tin, then season and bake in the oven for 20 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Once the mince is cooked, split it evenly between the softened peppers. Sprinkle with grated cheese if you wish to.
  6. Step 6: Put back in the oven for 10 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Before serving, scatter some herb leaves (e.g. basil or parsley).

Enjoy your Stuffed Peppers.
