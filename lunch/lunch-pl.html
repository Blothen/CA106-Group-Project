Polish Rosół

Ingredients

  • A whole chicken
  • 2 carrots
  • roughly 4 parsley leaves
  • 1 leek
  • 1 whole onion
  • whole peppercorn
  • celeriac
  • water

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cut the cucumbers, pickles and radishes into short, thin strips.
  2. Step 2: Finely slice the potatoes and eggs. Finely chop the scallions or chives and the dill.
  3. Step 3: Chop or grate the pickled beets if they are not already grated. Reserve the beet brine.
  4. Step 4: If you’re serving the soup in one large bowl, combine the kefir and beet brine in the bowl, add the horseradish and mustard, and whisk thoroughly.
  5. Step 5: Add the cucumbers, pickles, radishes, potatoes, beets, chives/scallions and half the chopped egg and half the chopped dill. Reserve the other halves for garnish.
  6. Step 6: Let sit for 20 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Serve the soup sprinkled with the remaining chopped egg and dill.

Enjoy your rosół
