Romanian Chicken Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 1kg deboned chicken
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red peppers
  • 1 courgette
  • 2 medium red onions

Instructions,
Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Marinating Time: 2 hours

  1. Step 1: Cut the chicken into bite size pieces.
  2. Step 2: Make the marinade by mixing the salt, pepper, chilli flakes and dried thyme.
  3. Step 3: Mix the chicken with the marinade, then add the olive oil and lemon juice. Mix well and leave to marinate in the fridge for 2 hours.
  4. Step 4: Cut the vegetables in chunky pieces, all around the same size.
  5. Step 5: After the two hours have passed, start builging the kebabs. Start with a slice of onion, and then alternate meat with vegetables until you reach the top of the skewer.
  6. Step 6: Spray the chicken kebabs with olive oil.
  7. Step 7: Grill over the charcoal barbecue for around 3-4 minutes on each side.
  8. Step 8: Serve along with fries or salad.

Enjoy your Romanian Chicken Kebabs.
