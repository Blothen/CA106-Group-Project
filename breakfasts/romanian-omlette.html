Romanian Omlette

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of sunflower oil
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 2 mushrooms
  • 1/2 smoked, thin sausage
  • 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • A bit of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions,
Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes

  1. Step 1: Dice the onion, mushrooms and the bell pepper. Cut the sausage into small pieces.
  2. Step 2: Heat the oil in a medium frying pan, then add the onion, bell pepper and mushrooms.
  3. Step 3: Cook the vegetables for 1-2 minutes, then add the sausage pieces and cook for two more minutes.
  4. Step 4: Beat the eggs with the milk and add the mixture to the pan on medium heat.
  5. Step 5: Cover the pan with a lid, and wait until the upper part looks fluffy and slightly cooked.
  6. Step 6: Using a spatula, carefully flip the omlette and cook for two more minutes.
  7. Step 7: Transfer the omlette to a plate and top it off with shredded cheese. Serve it alongisde bread or vegetables of your choice

Enjoy your Romanian Omlette!
